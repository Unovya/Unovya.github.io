Car cost Calculator

I created this calculator to calculate the cost of any car you may wish to buy!
All you have to input is the: Base cost of the car, how long your loan time is,
the intrest rate on the car, and the miles per gallon in the city and highway.

In return, you recieve the Monthly payments, Total gas price for the city and highway, your personal property tax (PPT),
the price of driving for 1 day, 1 month, 1 year, 2 years, 3 years, and 4 years!
